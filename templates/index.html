Welcome to NYU Daily

NYU Daily is a web-based app that allows NYU students to add their schedules onto the site and keep track of what class they need to go to next. The app also tells the user what time they should start leaving for their next class if they want to make it to their class on time. Since NYU has intergerated Poly-NYU, the app will take live-MTA data and inform the student of any delays.
